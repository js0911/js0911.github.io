LeBron James for the Lakers of the NBA. Nicknamed "King James", he is widely considered one of the greatest players of all time.

1999 - 2003

LeBron started early in– St. Mary high school i and led the school to three state titles in their historic run.

JUNE 26, 2003

NBA Draft

In the 2003 NBA Draft, LeBron was selected first pick overall from the Cleveland Cavaliers and win the NBA Rookie of the Year Award.

2003 - 2010

Cleveland Cavaliers

LeBron helped lead the Cavs to three postseason appearances. In the 2007-2008 season, he won the NBA scoring title averaging 30 points per game.

2010 - 2014

Miami Heat

James joined the Miami Heat in the 2010-11 season and won back-to-back championships in 2012 and 2013 with Finals MVP awards.

2014 - 2018

Cleveland Cavaliers

In year two, LeBron led the Cavaliers back from a 3-1 deficit to win the franchise’s first championship and the city’s first title in more than 50 years.

2018 - PRESENT

Los Angeles Lakers

In 2018, LeBron joined the Los Angeles Lakers. Year 2019, The Lakers eliminated the Heat in Game 6 finals, which earned James fourth NBA championship and fourth Finals MVP award.
